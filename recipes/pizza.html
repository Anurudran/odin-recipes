Pizza

Description

This is a receipe to make the best pizza ever. Follow the steps exactly to be able to have a pizza thats looks as great as the image above!

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  1. Make the dough circular
  2. Add the sauce and cheese on top of it
  3. Add the pizza into the oven for 15 mins
  4. Once the pizza is hot and ready, cut it into as many slices you see fit and then you can enjoy!!
